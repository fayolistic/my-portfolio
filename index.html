OUR STORY

PARALAX DINING is a fitting tribute to the timeless love of people for food. It’s a living celebration of the riches of tastes from all over the world. We serve inspired plates – built with fresh ingredients from across the country, and finessed with mastery. You and your esteemed peers are most welcome to our restraunt located in the the heart of the country. The vision of our Chef Nick Anderer and Director Natalie Johnson, thoughtfully reimagines India's rich culinary history through comforting, eclectic cuisine with deep Continental influences. The restaurant's design evokes warmth and a casual elegance.
